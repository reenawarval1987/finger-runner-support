Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Finger Runner?

Control your runner by tapping and reacting quickly to avoid obstacles and keep running as long as possible.

Does the game support Game Center?

Yes. Finger Runner supports Apple Game Center leaderboards so you can compete with friends and players worldwide.

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes. The game offers optional purchases for special runner shoes that allow you to customize your character.

Can I play the game offline?

Yes. Most gameplay features work without an internet connection.
