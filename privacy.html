Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Finger Runner handles user information. By using the game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection

Finger Runner does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

However, the game may collect anonymous data to improve the gaming experience.

Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Apple Game Center (leaderboards and achievements)
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services

These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Finger Runner offers optional in-app purchases that allow players to buy special shoes for their runner.

All purchases are processed securely through the Apple App Store. Finger Runner does not collect or store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

Finger Runner does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.
